About Us

Rasa Dewata

Bali Island is one of the tourist destinations in Indonesia which is most visited by tourists both from within and outside the country. In the tourism sector, especially in Bali, the role of restaurants is very important in creating a satisfying culinary experience for tourists. However, with the large choice of restaurants in Bali and tourists' limited time, tourists often face difficulties in finding a place to eat that suits their tastes and needs. This can result in a less than satisfactory dining experience and reduce tourists' overall level of satisfaction during their holiday. It is known that tourists' culinary preferences are very diverse, from looking for authentic local food to trying international cuisine. Factors such as price, food quality, restaurant atmosphere, and online reviews are the main considerations for tourists when choosing where to eat in Bali. Apart from that, there are differences in culinary preferences between domestic and international tourists as well as between tourists with different holiday destinations. Restaurant Recommendation

- Kopi Gayo

Kopi Gayo berasal dari daerah Aceh, tepatnya di Gayo Lues, dan dikenal dengan rasa yang kuat, asam yang rendah, serta aroma herbal dan rempah-rempah.

- Kopi Bali

Kopi Bali tumbuh di pulau Bali dan biasanya memiliki rasa yang halus, asam yang rendah, serta aroma yang lebih ringan daripada beberapa jenis kopi lainnya.

- Kopi Kopi-Lintong

Kopi Lintong berasal dari Sumatra Utara dan memiliki rasa yang kuat dengan asam yang rendah, serta aroma yang unik.

- Kopi Papua

Kopi Papua ditanam di daerah Papua dan sering memiliki rasa yang kuat, asam yang seimbang, serta karakteristik rasa yang beragam tergantung pada lokasi pertumbuhan.
